MINIMAL FASHION

Less is more.

When it comes to minimalist style, it’s not necessarily what you wear, but how you wear it. Take the quiet luxury fashion trend, for instance. It's the latest example of how a less is more approach to dressing can earn you major sartorial clout. Celebrities have been championing the look for years thanks to its timeless nature and wearability. No flashy fabrics or ostentatious silhouettes here. Still,to keep minimalist style fresh and relevant, it has to be reimagined—while staying true to its core identity. Fortunately for us, a few wardrobe basics and some fashion know-how are all you need to make a statement. To prove it to you, we asked fashion experts to share their best advice for wearing this simple yet stylish aesthetic.
